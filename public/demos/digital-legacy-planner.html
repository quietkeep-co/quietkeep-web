Digital Executor Instruction Sheet

This compiles your account map, devices, subscriptions, key people, and wishes into one printable packet: “What My Family Needs to Know About My Digital Life.” Print it, store it with your estate documents, and tell your digital executor where it lives.

The Instruction Sheet is a personal guide for your family, not a legal document, and it contains no passwords by design. Whether a digital executor has formal authority varies by state and by each platform's terms. For legal questions, consult a licensed attorney.
